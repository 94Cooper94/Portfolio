About Me:

Genesis

Thank you for taking a look at my portfolio page. My name is Cooper Ahearn-- I was born in New Orleans to parents Julie & Luke Ahearn. Although I consider myself a socialite, that wasn't always the case. I was a *very* shy child and almost exclusively found solace buried in technology. Relocating to the gorgeous Monterey, CA at the young age of six exacerbated the dread of dealing with others-- leaving me to further depend on technology to cheer me up. Electing to spend recesses, lunches, and any spare moment outide of school delving deeper into the world of computation further ingrained my commitment & gratitude towards tech.

High school gave me the much needed opportunity to re-invent myself as a \normal\ member of society. I joined clubs, got involved in sports, but one thing remained consistent-- my unwavering love of tech. Passively continuing my exploration of the field led me to build numerous computers, host countless servers, & grapple with front-end web development as early as 14 years of age.





Revelations

College truly opened the doors enabling me to push the range of my interests further than before. I enrolled in classes for debugging, web development, data-structures & analytics, and physics of computation. Following sixteen consecutive college quarters, Summers included, I graduated from the University of Oregon with a Bachelor's in Psychology.

I persisted in helping friends and coworkers construct computers, troubleshoot software, & design websites while waiting tables immediately following graduation. After three years of growth in an industy I lacked passion, I mustered the courage to risk the comfortable life I'd built and finally achieve my dream. Moved from Oregon to Texas, enrolled in an intensive full-time programming bootcamp through the University of Texas, and have been relentless in my pursuit of mastery. Have a look at the portfolio section below to see a few samples of my work, or follow my GitHub and connect with me on LinkedIn to stay up-to-date.
