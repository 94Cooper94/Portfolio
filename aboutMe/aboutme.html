About Me:

Cooper contemplates the technology behind the scenes that runs global Disney.

Born in New Orleans to artists Julie & Luke Ahearn, Cooper quickly found himself surrounded by modes of self-expression. At age 6, Cooper's family relocated to Monterey, CA. Having to switch schools numerous times, Cooper found most solace in video games.

In elementary school, Cooper would often sneak his Game Boy Color onto the playground or would spend his recesses and lunches in the classroom / library to play web browser games. This evolved in the Winter of '05, Cooper's fifth grade year, after getting into World of Warcraft.

From that point forward, Cooper found upgrading his computer to remain relevant irresistable. Understanding server protocol, navigating directories, understanding the logic behind firewalls-- everything tech related fascinated Cooper.
